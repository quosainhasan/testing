Welcome To Shia Educational Association

It was circa 1994 that the ‘Shia Educational Association’ (SEA) came into being as a brainchild of some intellectuals of the community who felt the need to eliminate illiteracy from the society and they selected education as a tool in achieving this goal. They were led by Late Dr. S. Agha Hadi Masih, Head, Department of Zoology, Shia Post Graduate College, Lucknow.

Dear Team members, Shia Educational Association I know Shia Educational Association (SEA) from last few years. I deeply appreciate every member of this team for their kind services, providing Education, and food to the needy people. During Covid you people has provided Oxygen gas cylinders in many areas of old Lucknow to Covid 19 infected patients and has saved precious lives. I wish good luck for their relentless efforts, dedication, and hard work toward the humanity. Thank you

Faizan Haider Khan Senior Scientist, Biosensia Ltd. Dublin 18, Ireland

In the famous letter Imam Ali (a.s.) wrote to Malik al-Ashtar, he described people as either your brother in religion or one like you in creation. I am delighted to see Shia Educational Association (SEA) inculcating this teaching of Imam Ali (a.s.) and assisting people in need without reservation. Although SEA resources are limited, the impact is enormous - there is nothing better than fulfilling people's educational needs. Therefore, I congratulate the SEA team for their tireless efforts and encourage people to support the SEA in any way possible. Thank you

Ata Abbas Research Scientist, Case Western Reserve University Cleveland, OH 44106
